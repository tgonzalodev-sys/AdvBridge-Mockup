# Product Requirements

## Product name
AdvertMind Commercial Bridge (ACB)

## Purpose
ACB is an internal operational web app that connects commercial information with administrative visibility.
Its purpose is to make client structure, contract scope, product assignments, expected billing, and invoiced control easy to see and maintain.

## Primary users
- Sales leadership
- Administrative leadership
- Back-office billing/control users

## Core value
One internal source of truth for:
- client hierarchy
- subsidiaries
- products
- contracts
- contract-product configuration
- billing concepts
- invoiced control

## Phase 1 in scope
- Client hierarchy
- Subsidiary working context
- Product catalog
- Contracts
- Contract-product lines
- Billing concepts
- Mark-as-invoiced workflow
- History for critical commercial evolution
- Locking for billed and past-period items

## Deferred features
- Contacts
- Invoice management system
- Dashboard/reporting
- Read-only combined subsidiary view
- Authentication/permissions
- Customer Success module

## Product principles
- Clarity over cleverness
- Operational speed over feature volume
- Maintainability over optimization
- Explicit rules over hidden logic
- Strong validation over loose data entry
