# Deferred Future Features

This document lists everything intentionally left out of phase 1, including items explicitly deferred and items that should remain deferred by design.

## Explicitly deferred
- Contact management
- Full invoice management system
- Dashboard and reporting screens
- Read-only aggregate view across both subsidiaries
- Authentication and permissions
- Customer Success module
- Other department-specific modules
- Combined cross-subsidiary editable views

## Strongly recommended to keep deferred
- Multi-tenant architecture
- ERP integrations
- Automatic currency conversion
- Workflow engine / approvals system
- Field-level permissions
- Audit actor tracking
- Mandatory change reasons
- Rich contract analytics / forecasting
- Full notification system
- External client portal
- Fine-grained role management
- Historical comparison dashboards
- Advanced billing reconciliation
- Tax logic / fiscal invoicing logic
- Product availability by subsidiary
- Product-level operational support/onboarding models
- Contract attachment/document storage workflows

## Future candidates for later phases
### Commercial / CS
- account health tracking
- onboarding status
- renewal risk
- CSM ownership
- service notes on Madre/Hija

### Operational
- read-only combined subsidiary view
- richer history screens
- contract comparison view
- billing exception center
- contract end/discount expiry alerts

### Governance / security
- authentication
- module/section permissions
- actor tracking
- approval flows for dangerous edits
